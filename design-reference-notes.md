# TDev Digital — Visual Reference Notes

The new TDev Digital website should borrow direction from premium digital agency and creative technology sites, but should not copy any single reference.

## Desired blend

- Dark editorial impact from Blue Frontier-style agency sites
- Technical grid/detail from Platform-style web development sites
- Human, crafted copy and spacing from Paddle Creative-style sites
- Bold featured work treatment from Atomic-style agency sites
- Serious AI/data/software credibility from Artefact-style sites

## Overall feel

The site should feel like a small but highly capable digital studio.

It should communicate:
- technical capability
- design taste
- practical business value
- real project experience
- modern software/web app credibility

It should not feel:
- like a cheap local website designer
- like a generic AI SaaS landing page
- like a crypto/futuristic template
- like a massive fake agency
- like a WordPress theme

## Visual rules

- Use a dark base, but break the page with selected light or colour-block sections.
- Use large typography and strong whitespace.
- Let real project images do more work than abstract decoration.
- Use one strong accent colour, not rainbow gradients.
- Avoid generic glowing blobs.
- Avoid repeated 3-column card grids.
- Avoid over-rounded cards.
- Avoid fake dashboard mockups unless they represent real work.
- Use subtle technical details: grid lines, coordinate marks, small labels, thin rules, index numbers.
- Make layout feel composed, editorial and intentionally asymmetric.

## Homepage concept

Theme:
"Built systems, not just websites."

Structure:
1. Hero
2. Credibility strip
3. Selected Work
4. Capabilities
5. Process
6. CTA

The work section should be the visual centrepiece of the page.

## Copy style

Use direct, grounded language.

Avoid:
- transform your digital presence
- elevate your brand
- innovative solutions
- cutting-edge
- seamless experiences
- bespoke digital solutions
- unlock potential

Prefer:
- build
- design
- launch
- improve
- automate
- websites
- web apps
- AI systems
- digital infrastructure
- practical
- clear
- robust
- measurable
